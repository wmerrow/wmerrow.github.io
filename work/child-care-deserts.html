    Design Notes

    The default view shows a choropleth and summary graphics.

    After selecting a state, a user is taken to the state page, where they can view census tract level information and adjust filters to compare urban, suburban, and rural areas.

    The user can zoom in and explore the locations of child care centers in their city or neighborhood.

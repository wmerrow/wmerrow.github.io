    A CFR project

    https://www.cfr.org/article/venezuelan-exodus

    My role: lead visualization design

    Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Excel

    Design Notes

    The CFR editorial team wanted to put out a piece on the crisis in Venezuela in a short timeframe, so we decided to create a larger infographic with some brief accompanying text rather than a traditional article. I used bubbles to represent the number of refugees in destination countries, with data values mapped to bubble area. I added arrows spreading out from Venezuela with the goal of giving users an immediate understanding of the issue at hand - mass exodus - before reading the details of the graphic. I added an area chart in an empty part of the map in order to show the dramatic change over time.

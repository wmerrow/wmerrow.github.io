    • Council on Foreign Relations - Visualizing 2019

    • Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Excel
    • https://www.cfr.org/article/visualizing-2019-trends-watch

    This article used data visualization to highlight five important trends for the coming year. I coordinated with research fellows and editorial staff to design graphics that tell each story succinctly.

    For this graphic, we started with a broad idea of mapping the decline of coal and the rise of renewable energy. I suggested two maps showing closing and opening plants in order to show all the desired variables while clearly communicating the dramatic shift.

    In this graphic on the trade war with China, annotations are used to aid the user in understanding how to read the chart, and present the information as a story unfolding over the course of the past year.

    All data visualizations at CFR conforms to a graphic style guide to ensure a consistent look and feel and use of best practices across all graphics.

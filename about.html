    • I'm an information designer who is passionate about data visualization and knowledgable about data analysis, UX, visual design, and front-end web development. I work at Graphicacy, a web development and design studio in Washington, DC where I help create interactive data visualizations for nonprofits and think tanks.

    I believe in creating things that are clear, something I have focused on in all of my work - graphics, writing, and communication in general. I enjoy beautiful visualizations, but I think many are really data art.

    I previously worked in international development, as Special Assistant to the President at InterAction and then as a Princeton in Latin America Fellow with the DREAM Project in the Dominican Republic. I graduated from Tufts University with a B.A. in International Relations, and have been adding more data and design work to my roles since then. Other passions include folk music, board game design, and progressive politics.
